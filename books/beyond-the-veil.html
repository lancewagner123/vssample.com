Volume II

Beyond the Veil

Past the threshold, the watchers have kingdoms of their own.

A dark elf sentinel stands on the Umbral Cliffs above a broken valley and sees the next invasion before the surface ever names it. To stop it, she must guide an outsider through Thornwake Swamp, noble courts, war altars, and a prophecy written in ash.

GenreEpic dark fantasy

Story Hook

Beyond the Veil shifts from survival to discovery as the route bends from the Umbral Cliffs into Thornwake Swamp. The mystery is no longer only what lies behind the barrier, but who has been guarding it and why the guardians are beginning to fracture.

Key Threads

Expect cliffside citadels above the western sea, Thornwake's drowned paths, dark elf politics, oath magic, border wars, forbidden gates, and a reluctant alliance between people who should have remained enemies.

Reader Response Draft

Position this volume for readers who liked the first book's atmosphere but want a broader world, sharper character alliances, and a more direct sense of what is waiting beyond the swamp.
