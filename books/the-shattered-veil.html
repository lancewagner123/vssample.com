The Shattered Veil

The barrier breaks where the oldest bones are buried.

Below the Shattered Gate and the ruined cities around Slatewater Deep, a deep dragon crawls through catacombs lined with the remains of adventurers who went looking for glory and found the machinery of the Veil instead.

Story Hook

The Shattered Veil brings the series underground into the Drowned Catacombs. These ruins below Slatewater Deep are not ruins at all; they are a lock. The dragon is not simply a monster; it is what the lock was built to contain.

Key Threads

Adventurer skeletons, broken shields, the Shattered Gate, green-lit fissures, ancient dragon speech, buried machinery, and the first full explanation of why the Veil was made to fail.

Reader Response Draft

Position this volume for readers who want the biggest monster set piece yet, answers to the series mystery, and a darker dungeon-crawl tone with high fantasy consequences.
