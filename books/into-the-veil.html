Volume I

Into the Veil

The threshold opens in a swamp where the dead still keep watch.

When a scarred scout follows a green fire into the marsh, he finds a hidden realm sealed behind rot, bone, and old vows. To return, he must bargain with a skull-crowned warlord who knows his name before he speaks it.

GenreDark fantasy / LitRPG-adjacent

Story Hook

Into the Veil introduces the crossing: a place where swamps swallow roads, armor is scavenged from forgotten heroes, and every creature seems to be guarding a rule the living never learned.

Key Threads

A cursed map, a bone-shield guardian, a faction of oathbreakers, and the first clue that the Veil is not a wall. It is a wound that something keeps reopening.

Reader Response Draft

Position this volume for readers who respond to survival fantasy, grim portals, monstrous set pieces, and a hero learning the rules of a hostile world one mistake at a time.
