Series

The Veil

Three volumes, one widening breach between worlds.

The Veil follows a dark fantasy progression from a first crossing into a cursed threshold, to the kingdoms beyond it, to the wet catacombs where the barrier finally begins to fracture.

Series Hook

The Veil is built around forbidden crossings, fractured rules, and characters who learn that the border between worlds was never as sealed as people believed.

Reading Order

Begin with Into the Veil, continue into Beyond the Veil, then descend into The Shattered Veil for the largest reveal of the current arc.
