The Veil series

Three volumes, one widening breach between worlds.

The Veil series follows a dark fantasy arc from first contact with a cursed threshold, to the dangerous powers beyond it, to the catacombs where the barrier finally begins to fracture. Story details below are draft promotional copy for author review.

Volume I

Into the Veil

A mercenary scout crosses into a swamp-bound realm where old gods rot, armor remembers its dead, and every step deeper asks for a price.

About the author

Travis Wagner writes fantasy, science fiction, and LitRPG-inspired fiction.

Travis Wagner is an author of fantasy, science fiction, and LitRPG-inspired fiction. Born in Arizona and now based in Tennessee, he draws from a lifelong interest in speculative storytelling, immersive worlds, and adventure-driven spirit, which shape the stories he writes today.

Veiled Studios is the official home for his books, release announcements, reader updates, and community-focused fiction projects. His work is written for readers who enjoy imaginative worlds, hidden thresholds, and exciting stories that blend mystery, adventure, and exhilaration.
